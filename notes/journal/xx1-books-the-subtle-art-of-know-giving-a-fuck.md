# 📘 The Subtle Art of Not Giving a F\*ck

You always give a f\*ck about something—so choose carefully what actually deserves your attention and energy.

---

## 🔑 Principles

### 1. Not giving a f\*ck ≠ apathy

- It’s **selective caring**, not indifference
- Care deeply about what truly matters, ignore the rest

---

### 2. Choose your problems

- Life is always problems → happiness comes from **solving better problems**
- Good life = choosing problems you enjoy dealing with

---

### 3. Embrace responsibility

- You may not control what happens
- But you **always control your response**
- Growth = owning your situation

---

### 4. Failure is the way forward

- Failure isn’t the opposite of success—it’s part of it
- Progress = iteration, not perfection

---

### 5. Question your beliefs

- Many beliefs are outdated or wrong
- Be willing to say: _“Maybe I’m wrong”_
- Flexibility > certainty

---

### 6. You’re not special

- Everyone struggles, everyone is flawed
- Accepting this reduces entitlement and anxiety

---

### 7. Values determine your life

Good values:

- Reality-based
- Controllable
- Constructive

Bad values:

- External validation
- Pleasure-only focus
- Always being right

---

### 8. Certainty is the enemy of growth

- Doubt drives learning
- Confidence comes from action, not knowing everything

---

### 9. Death gives life meaning

- Awareness of mortality clarifies priorities
- Forces focus on what actually matters

---

## 🧭 One-Line Summary

> Stop trying to feel good all the time—focus on what’s worth struggling for.
