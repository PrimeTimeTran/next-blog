# 🧠 Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS) — Cheat Sheet

## 🔑 Core Idea

Run your business on a **simple, structured system** that aligns:

- Vision
- People
- Execution
- Data

---

## 🧩 The 6 Key Components

### 1. Vision

Get everyone aligned on where you're going.

- Define:

  - Core values
  - Core focus (purpose + niche)
  - 10-year target
  - Marketing strategy
  - 3-year picture
  - 1-year plan
  - Quarterly priorities (“Rocks”)

---

### 2. People

Right people, right seats.

- Use **GWC Framework**:

  - **Gets it** (understands role)
  - **Wants it** (motivated)
  - **Capacity** (has ability)

- Tools:

  - Accountability chart (not org chart)
  - Core values filter

---

### 3. Data

Manage with numbers, not feelings.

- Create a **Scorecard**:

  - 5–15 weekly metrics
  - Each has an owner
  - Clear “on track / off track”

---

### 4. Issues

Solve problems at the root.

- Use **IDS**:

  - **Identify** the real issue
  - **Discuss** openly
  - **Solve** with action

- Maintain an **Issues List** at all levels

---

### 5. Process

Document and follow what works.

- Identify **core processes**
- Document at high level (20% effort → 80% clarity)
- Ensure everyone follows them

---

### 6. Traction

Execute with discipline.

- Set **Rocks (quarterly goals)**
- Weekly **Level 10 Meetings**
- Use a **meeting pulse**

---

## 🪨 Rocks (Quarterly Priorities)

- 3–7 most important goals per quarter
- Must be:

  - Specific
  - Measurable
  - Owned by one person

---

## 📊 Scorecard Rules

- Weekly numbers only
- Predictive > historical
- Owner per metric
- Example:

  - Leads generated
  - Sales calls booked
  - Revenue

---

## 🧭 Level 10 Meeting Structure (90 min)

1. Segue (5 min) – good news
2. Scorecard (5 min)
3. Rocks (5 min)
4. Customer/Employee headlines (5 min)
5. To-do list (5 min)
6. IDS (60 min) ← most important
7. Conclude (5 min)

---

## ⚙️ Accountability Chart

- Focus on **functions**, not titles
- Clear ownership:

  - Visionary
  - Integrator
  - Departments (Sales, Marketing, Ops, Finance)

---

## 🎯 Core Principles (TL;DR)

- Simplicity > complexity
- Clarity > assumptions
- Discipline > motivation
- Accountability > consensus
- Data > opinions
- Solve root problems, not symptoms

---

## 🧠 Mental Models

- “What gets measured gets managed”
- “Right people, right seats”
- “Vision without traction = hallucination”
- “You can’t solve what you won’t identify”

---

## 🚀 How to Start (Quick Path)

1. Define Vision
2. Build Accountability Chart
3. Set Rocks
4. Create Scorecard
5. Run weekly Level 10s
6. Track + solve issues consistently
