## Workout For Men

1. **Bodyweight Windmill** (Abs, Obliques, Glutes, Hamstrings). With arms extended at the sides, touch opposite side toe. Alternate

2. **Standing Ab Twist** (Obliques, Glutes). With hands clasped forward, turn left and right fully extending.

3. **Diagonal Punc**h (Delts, Pecs, Triceps, Obliques, Glutes, Hamstrings, Calves). Punch across the body. Alternating

4. **Seated Twist** (Abs, Obliques). From Wide Angled Seated twist the arm to touch the opposite toe. Alternate

5. **Sitting Windshield Wipers** (Abs, Obliques). Hands behind you on the ground. Knees touching and at 90 degrees, lift and move them left to right

6. **Front Plank to Toe Tap** (Delts, Pecs, Abs, Obliques, Erector spinae, Glutes, Quads). From plank lift leg and move up toward chest and touch knee with opposite hand.

7. **Elbow to Knee Side Plank** Crunch R (Abs, Obliques, Glutes, Quads, Hamstrings)

8. **Elbow to Knee Side Plank** Crunch L (Abs, Obliques, Glutes, Quads, Hamstrings)

9. **Twist Crunch** (Abs, Obliques, Quads)

10. **Mountain Climber** Cross (Delts, Pecs, Abs, Obliques, Erector spinae, Glutes, Quads, Hamstrings). From plank lift leg and turn it underneath belly toward opposite side.
