# Career Hub

## Purpose

A central hub for developing career skills, interview readiness, and professional growth in software engineering and related technical roles.

This includes preparation for coding interviews, system design interviews, and communication-based evaluations like white-boarding.

---

## Overview

Career development can be broken into three major skill layers:

- Technical execution → solving problems (coding, data structures, algorithms)
- Communication under pressure → explaining solutions clearly (white-boarding)
- System thinking → designing scalable systems (architecture & trade-offs)

## How to Think About Career Growth

Career progression in technical roles is less about isolated knowledge and more about:

- Translating problems into structured approaches
- Communicating trade-offs clearly
- Moving from code-level thinking → system-level thinking
- Practicing under constraints (time, ambiguity, incomplete requirements)
