# Discrete Math

> Core idea: Math for reasoning about discrete structures (finite, countable, logical systems)

---

# 1. Logic & Proofs

## Topics

- Propositions
- Logical operators (AND, OR, NOT, →, ↔)
- Truth tables
- Logical equivalence
- Quantifiers (∀, ∃)

## Proof Techniques

- Direct proof
- Proof by contradiction
- Proof by contrapositive
- Mathematical induction

## Why it matters

- Foundation for correctness in algorithms
- Used in invariants and formal reasoning

# 2. Set Theory

## Topics

- Sets and subsets
- Union, intersection, difference
- Power sets
- Cartesian products

## Key ideas

- Sets as collections of distinct elements
- Operations mirror logic (AND ↔ intersection, OR ↔ union)

## Why it matters

- Data structures (sets, maps)
- Database queries
- Probability

## Functions

- Mapping inputs → outputs
