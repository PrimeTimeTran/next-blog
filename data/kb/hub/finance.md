# 💰 Finance Hub

Finance is the system of **allocating capital across time under uncertainty**, combining economics, accounting, markets, and optimization to turn information into decisions.

---

# 🧠 0. Finance System Model (How everything connects)

Finance operates as a pipeline:

## 🧩 Core flow

```text
Accounting → Markets → Institutions → Capital Allocation → Risk Control → Valuation → Outcomes
```

### Meaning:

- Accounting → records reality
- Markets → price reality
- Institutions → move capital
- Corporate Finance → decides internal capital use
- Investments → allocates capital externally
- Risk → constrains uncertainty
- Valuation → translates expectations into price
