# ⏳ Time Value of Money (TVM) Hub

Time Value of Money is the principle that:

> A dollar today is worth more than a dollar in the future due to its earning potential.

---

# 🧠 Core Idea

Money grows over time because it can be:

- invested
- compounded
- reinvested

# 🔑 Key Variables

- **PV** = Present Value (today’s money)
- **FV** = Future Value (future money)
- **r** = interest rate
- **t** = time
- **n** = number of compounding periods

# 🧩 Intuition

TVM is driven by:

- opportunity cost
- inflation
- risk
- reinvestment potential

---

# 💰 Discounting vs Compounding

## Compounding (forward in time)

> grow money into the future

## Discounting (backward in time)

> convert future money into today’s value

---

# 📊 Applications

## Corporate Finance

- capital budgeting (NPV)
- project evaluation
- valuation of cash flows

## Investments

- bond pricing
- stock valuation
- retirement planning

- loan amortization

# 🧠 Key Insight

> Value today is determined by all future cash flows discounted back to the present.

---

# ⚖️ Why TVM matters

Without TVM:

- no investment comparison
- no rational pricing of assets
- no capital allocation framework
