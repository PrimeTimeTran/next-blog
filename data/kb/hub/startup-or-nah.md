# 🚀 Startup Decision Hub

> What to evaluate before starting or joining a startup.

---

# 🧠 1. Problem & Market

- [[Market Research]]
- [[Product Market Fit(PMF)]]
  - [[executive-summary|Executive Summary]]
  - [[business-model-canvas|Business Model Canvas]]

### Key Questions

- Is this a real problem or a “nice to have”?
- Who cares enough to pay?
- Is the market growing or dying?
- Are there existing solutions?

---

# 📦 2. Product & Value

- [[Product Market Fit(PMF)]]
- [[Content Marketing]] (for distribution validation)

### Key Questions

- Does the product actually solve the problem?
- Is there early traction or just ideas?
- Is this 10x better or marginally better?

---

# 📈 3. Growth & Distribution

- [[Marketing]]
  - [[Social Media Marketing]]
  - [[Content Marketing]]
  - [[Growth Hacking]]

### Key Questions

- How will users be acquired?
- Is there a repeatable growth channel?
- Is growth organic or paid?

---

# 💰 4. Business Model

- [[business-model-canvas|Business Model Canvas]]

### Key Questions

- How does this make money?
- Are margins good?
- Is revenue predictable?

---

# 🧾 5. Fundraising & Financials

- [[Fundraising]]
- [[Equity]]
  - [[Cap Table]]
  - [[Negotiation]]

### Key Questions

- How much runway is left?
- What are the funding plans?
- What’s the dilution risk?
- Is the cap table clean?

---

# 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 6. Team & Founders

- [[Co Founders]]
- [[Team]]
  - [[behavioral-interview-questions|Questions]]

### Key Questions

- Do founders have domain expertise?
- Can they execute?
- Any red flags in behavior or history?
- Would I trust them under stress?

---

# ⚖️ 7. Role & Personal Fit

- [[behavioral-interview-questions|Questions]]

### Key Questions

- What exactly is my role?
- Do I have ownership or just tasks?
- What am I learning here?
- What happens if this fails?

---

# ⚠️ 8. Risk Assessment

### Key Questions

- What are the biggest failure points?
- Is this idea dependent on one thing going right?
- Is timing favorable?

---

# 🧭 9. Personal Decision Filter

### Key Questions

- Does this align with my goals?
- Is the upside worth the risk?
- Would I regret not doing this?

---

# 📌 Core Insight

A startup is not just:

- an idea
- a product
- a team

It is a combination of:

> **market + timing + execution + people + distribution + capital**

---

# 🧠 Minimal Checklist

- [ ] Real problem exists
- [ ] Market is large or growing
- [ ] Product shows traction
- [ ] Clear path to growth
- [ ] Business model makes sense
- [ ] Founders are strong
- [ ] Equity is fair
- [ ] Risk is understood
