# Topic

Differentiating is important to calculus. It's how we determine the rate of change.
Solve these problems to prove your knowledge of the rules of differentiation.

# 1.0.1 Problem — ...

**Instructions... Initial..**

$$
f(x) = 7
$$

**Work**

$$
f(x) = \left\{\begin{aligned}
x < 10 \\
x > 0
\end{aligned}\right.
$$

## Solution

$$
f'(x) = 0
$$

**Reference**
[[constant-rule]]
