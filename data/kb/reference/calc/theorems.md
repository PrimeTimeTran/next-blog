# 📘 Calculus 1–3 Core Theorems Cheat Sheet

---

# 🧠 Calc 1 — Single Variable Foundations

---

## 1. Limit Laws

If limits exist, algebra works normally (addition, multiplication, division rules apply).

**Idea:**

> Limits can be evaluated piece-by-piece

---

## 2. Intermediate Value Theorem (IVT)

If ( f ) is continuous on ([a,b]), then it takes every value between ( f(a) ) and ( f(b) ).

**Meaning:**

> No jumps → no skipped values

---

## 3. Extreme Value Theorem (EVT)

If ( f ) is continuous on a closed interval ([a,b]), then it attains:

- a maximum
- a minimum

---

## 4. Mean Value Theorem (MVT)

$$
f'(c) = \frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}
$$

**Meaning:**

> At some point, instantaneous slope equals average slope

---

# 🔁 Calc 2 — Integrals & Series

---

## 5. Fundamental Theorem of Calculus (FTC I)

$$
\frac{d}{dx} \int_a^x f(t),dt = f(x)
$$

**Meaning:**

> Differentiation undoes accumulation

---

## 6. Fundamental Theorem of Calculus (FTC II)

$$
\int_a^b f(x),dx = F(b) - F(a)
$$

**Meaning:**

> Definite integral = net change of antiderivative

---

## 7. Substitution Rule (u-sub)

$$
\int f(g(x))g'(x),dx = \int f(u),du
$$

**Meaning:**

> Reverse chain rule

---

## 8. Integration by Parts

$$
\int u,dv = uv - \int v,du
$$

**Meaning:**

> Reverse product rule

---

# 🌊 Calc 3 — Multivariable + Vector Calculus

---

## 9. Gradient Theorem

$$
\int_C \nabla f \cdot d\mathbf{r} = f(B) - f(A)
$$

**Meaning:**

> Line integral of gradient depends only on endpoints

---

## 10. Green’s Theorem

(2D circulation ↔ area)

**Idea:**

> Line integral around a curve = double integral over region

---

## 11. Stokes’ Theorem

(3D generalization of Green’s Theorem)

**Idea:**

> Surface curl ↔ boundary circulation

---

## 12. Divergence Theorem (Gauss)

$$
\iint_{\partial V} \mathbf{F} \cdot d\mathbf{S} = \iiint_V (\nabla \cdot \mathbf{F}),dV
$$

**Meaning:**

> Flux through boundary = total divergence inside

---

# 🧭 Big Picture

---

## Derivatives vs Integrals

- Derivative → local change
- Integral → accumulation

---

## Core Calc 3 theme

- Gradient → direction of steepest increase
- Curl → rotation
- Divergence → source/sink behavior

---

# 🧠 One-line summary

- Calc 1: change on a line
- Calc 2: accumulation over intervals
- Calc 3: change + accumulation in space
