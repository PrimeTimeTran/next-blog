> Low d-high minus high d-low, over the square of what's below

| Rule Component             | Meaning                               |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------- |
| Low                        | The bottom function, $g(x)$           |
| D-High                     | The derivative of the top, $f'(x)$    |
| High                       | The top function, $f(x)$              |
| D-Low                      | The derivative of the bottom, $g'(x)$ |
| The square of what's below | $[g(x)]^2$                            |
