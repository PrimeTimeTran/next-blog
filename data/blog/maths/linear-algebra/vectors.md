- Vector

  $$
  \overline{a} = \begin{bmatrix} 5 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix}
  $$

- Other Notation

  $$
  \overline{v} = \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \end{bmatrix}
  $$

  $$
  \overline{v} = \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \\ z \\ w \end{bmatrix}
  $$

  $$
  \overline{v} = \begin{bmatrix} v_{x} \\ v_{y} \\ v_{z} \\ v_{w} \end{bmatrix}
  $$

  $$
  \overline{v} = \begin{bmatrix} v_{1} \\ v_{2} \\ ... \\ v_{n} \end{bmatrix}
  $$

- Scalar Operations

  $$
  \overline{a} \cdot x
  $$

  $$
  \overline{a} / x
  $$

  $$
  \overline{a} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}
  $$

  $$
  x = 3
  $$

  $$
  \overline{a} \cdot x = \begin{bmatrix} 6 \\ 3 \end{bmatrix}
  $$

The result of multiple is scaling the vector if the x is positive. If dividing it's shrinking. If it's a negative x than reversing.

- Vector Operations

  $$
  \overline{a} + \overline{b} = \begin{bmatrix} a_{x} + b_{x} \\ a_{y} + b_{y} \end{bmatrix}
  $$

  - Adding: new point.
  - Subtracting: calculate a vector from one point to another.

- Length/Magnitude/Norm

  $$
  \left|| \overline{v} \right||
  $$

  $$
  \left|| \overline{v} \right|| = \sqrt{v_{x}^{2} + v_{y}^{2}}
  $$

- Unit Vector Notation
- Dot Product/Scalar Product

  $$
  \overline{a} · \overline{b} = a_{x} b_{x} + a_{y} b_{y} + a_{z} b_{z}
  $$

  $$
  \overline{a} · \overline{b} = a_{1} b_{1} \space + a_{2} b_{2}\space  + \space ... \space  + \space  a_{n } b_{n}
  $$
